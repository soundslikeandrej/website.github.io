About

Giving a nod to the singer-songwriters of yesteryears Sounds like Andrej creates simple, straight to the point songs, content of which range from light-hearted observations to sincere inner reflections. Inspired by songwriters like Elliot Smith, the EP “Ordinary Life” was recorded in a room in Berlin, the sound of which reflects a “learn as you go” attitude towards production and songwriting. Currently Andrej is finishing up an LP which is due to be released soon.
